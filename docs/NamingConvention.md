- Image: `img`
- ImageRound: `imr`
- View: `viw`
- TouchableWithoutFeedback: `twf`
- TextInput: `txtip`
- Icon: `ico`
- Modal: 'mod'
- DropDown: `dpn`
- RadioButton: `rdbtn`
- TextButtonGroup: `txtbg`
